Strategic Product Recommendations

1. Radical Catalog Pruning

5,946 products (98.6%) have zero sales. This hurts site speed, SEO dilution, and user experience. Recommend: archive products with zero views AND zero sales for 6+ months. Start with the 1,190 unbranded products and the 439 products from Cassina/Santa & Cole/Marset that aren't selling. A leaner catalog of 500-800 curated products would dramatically improve conversion rate.

2. Panton Sandalye Expansion

At 0.45 units/day, Panton is your cash cow. Actions: (1) Create "Panton Lifestyle" landing page with room settings, (2) Bundle with Panton Çocuk for "parent + child" set at 10% discount, (3) Launch color-of-the-month Instagram campaigns, (4) Cross-sell with Hang It All (your #4 product). Combined, Panton + Panton Kids = €15.3K — protect and grow this franchise.

3. Eames Collection Strategy

Charles & Ray Eames designs generate €28.9K across 67 products — that's €431/product vs €12/product average. Create a dedicated "Eames World" collection page. Feature the Eames Tekli Koltuk (€12K single sale!), Elephant Tabure, Plastic chairs, and Hang It All together. The Eames heritage story resonates powerfully with your affluent Istanbul buyer.

4. Gift & Accessory Push

Accessories account for 145 units sold (65% of all items) but only 29% of revenue. These are gateway products. Key Ring Anahtarlık (0.29/day), Spirale Küllük (0.15/day) bring customers in. Strategy: (1) Create curated gift boxes (Küllük + Anahtarlık + Minyatür = "Design Lover's Gift"), (2) Position as entry-point for future furniture purchases, (3) Retarget accessory buyers with chair campaigns after 30 days.
