Marketing Director's Briefing
📈
What's Working
Revenue doubled year-over-year. 2025 hit €249K vs €123K in 2024 — a clean 102% growth with AOV jumping from €499 to €751. December 2025 alone did €95K (87 orders), making it the single strongest month in company history.

Repeat customers are your hidden engine. Just 20% of customers generate 44% of all revenue. These loyalists have significantly higher AOV and buy premium products. This is a major retention asset to double down on.

Panton Sandalye is the franchise product. Three color variants (Siyah, Beyaz, Bordo) together account for 300 units sold and €123K+ in revenue — roughly 24% of total sales from a single chair line.
⚠️
Where to Focus
€291K in abandoned orders. 242 cancelled or failed transactions — that's 56% of your successful revenue sitting on the table. Payment failures (iyzico rejections) and cart abandonment are the biggest leaks in the funnel.

Instagram traffic converts poorly. Only 7.4% of orders come from IG despite heavy ad spend. Revenue share is just 3.8%. The channel works for awareness but the conversion path from IG to purchase needs serious optimization.

February 2026 dropped hard. After a monster Dec-Jan, Feb pulled back to 40 orders (€39.7K). While AOV remained strong at €993, the volume drop suggests seasonal normalization or budget fatigue. March is tracking even lower.

More in half the time
The Katapult period (Sep–Dec 2025) generated 29% more revenue than the entire first 8 months of 2025 — in just 4 months. Even excluding the Dec holiday spike, Sep-Nov averaged €15.2K/month vs €13.6K/month pre-Katapult. The growth acceleration is real.
Strategic Recommendations
1. Fix the Cancellation Funnel
€291K in lost orders is your #1 opportunity. Implement abandoned cart emails, simplify checkout, and test payment alternatives beyond iyzico. Target: reduce cancellations by 30% = ~€87K recovered.
2. Build a Panton Campaign
With 300 units sold across colors, Panton is your hero product. Create a dedicated landing page, bundle deals (buy 2+ save X%), and seasonal color launches to keep momentum.
3. Activate Repeat Customers
124 repeat customers drive 44% of revenue. Launch a VIP program with early access, exclusive colors, or loyalty discounts. Convert even 10% of one-time buyers = ~€91K potential.
4. Rethink Instagram Strategy
IG drives only 3.8% of order revenue despite significant ad spend. Either optimize the IG-to-purchase funnel (better landing pages, retargeting) or reallocate budget toward Google where ROAS is stronger.
