Strategic Recommendations

1. Activate the 910 Browsers

59% of registered users never purchased. Build a 3-email welcome series: (1) brand story + bestsellers, (2) social proof from Istanbul designers, (3) first-purchase incentive (free shipping or 5% off). Expected conversion: 5-8% = 45-72 new customers at €608 AOV = €27K-44K incremental revenue.

2. Launch VIP Loyalty Program

Top 10 customers = 22.5% of revenue. Create a tiered program: Silver (2+ orders), Gold (€5K+ LTV), Platinum (€10K+ LTV). Offer early access to new collections, dedicated design consultations, and exclusive colorways. Goal: reduce 119-day repeat interval to 90 days and push repeat rate from 21% to 30%.

3. Cart Abandonment Automation

While 65% recovery is exceptional, the 8 unrecovered carts hold €604K. Implement: (1) automated email at 30 min (before the 46-min avg abandon point), (2) WhatsApp message at 2 hours for carts >€1K, (3) phone call within 24 hours for carts >€5K. The Eames Koltuk cart alone (€482K) justifies a dedicated sales follow-up.

4. Double Down on B2B Channel

184 B2B buyers generating €194K (37.5% of revenue) is a gold mine. Create a dedicated B2B portal with trade pricing, project-based ordering, and net-30 payment terms. Target interior designers, architects, and hospitality firms in Istanbul, Ankara, and İzmir. Oney Interior's €78K abandoned cart shows the appetite is there.
